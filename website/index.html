A Journey to Trust

A journery to trust is a series of short virtual reality films about a female protagonist attempting to find her life partner. This project is meant to educate women on how to safely look for the red flags when chosing their life partner. In a world where media aids the deceptions of personas, this media platformform will be used to expose these habits of deception. The use of VR forces the audience into an immersive experience where they become the characters they chose. Each charcter brings a differnt story of deception in love. With your senses being immersed into the new reality and your agency to act is remvoed, you are forced to experience the heavness of the storyline. All in the attempts of removing the emotion of regret without removing experience. The concept of this experience is to bridge between acknowledge and practice of love, to improve the safety of women and their hearts.
